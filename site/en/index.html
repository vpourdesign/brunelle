About

A North Shore real estate broker since 1992, I put the numbers on the table and we decide together.

I guide families, first-time buyers and investors in Sainte-Thérèse, Blainville, Rosemère and Lorraine through what is often the biggest financial decision of their lives. In concrete terms, that means three things: I know each neighbourhood well, I set a price that holds up thanks to recent comparable sales, and I stay transparent with you from the first call to the signing at the notary's.
